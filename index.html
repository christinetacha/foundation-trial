Strawberry Brown Butter Cupcakes

These delicious cupcakes were inspired by the chefs of New York City, where brown butter is used to enhance flavors of a wide range of dishes. Topped with vanilla bean frosting, with real strawberries blended in. Made entirely with organic and sustainably-grown ingredients.

Maple Chocolate Cupcakes

These cupcakes are made with chocolate ganache, and have a molten chocolate center. Topped with maple buttercream frosting. We use maple syrup directly imported from a small farm in Canada. Help support family-owned businesses with these tasty cupcakes. Made entirely with organic and sustainably-grown ingredients.

Raspberry Delite

These cupcakes pack a nutritrious punch, with frosting made from non-fat greek yogurt and stevia. Each cupcake has 10 grams of protein, and is rich in antioxidants. Remove the guilt from your daily indulgence. Made entirely with organic and sustainably-grown ingredients.
